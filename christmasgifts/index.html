Introduction

In order to simplify our Christmas festivities last year: my sister suggested that we could arrange things so each family member only gave and received one present each. At the time I wrote a command line program in Haskell to allocate each family member another one to give a present to. This page is essentially the same thing: but in the form of a web app in React.js

Usage

Use the form elements in the standard manner. Any participant can be revisited by clicking on their name. If a participant requires special assistance: you can nominate two or more helpers (whose names will appear in green). You can also remove participants by clicking the red Ã—.

Once you're satified with the list of participants: click the "Do allocations" button. Links will appear below or to the right depending on your screen size: you are recommended to download them and email each to the corresponding participant without opening.
